About the App

The Interview Preparation Assistant is designed to help you ace your job interviews. By simulating real interview scenarios and providing insightful feedback, it helps you:

  • Practice answering common interview questions.
  • Receive personalized feedback on your responses.
  • Articulate your skills and experiences more effectively.
  • Build confidence for your next interview.

Using advanced AI, powered by Langchain and the Groq LLM, our assistant acts as a dedicated interview coach, guiding you through practice sessions and helping you refine your answers.

How It Works

Simply click the "Start Talking" button within the web application to begin practicing. Speak naturally, as you would in a real interview. Once you stop, the assistant will analyze your response and provide constructive feedback.

The conversation history is maintained, allowing the assistant to provide contextually relevant advice and follow-up questions, just like a human interviewer.
